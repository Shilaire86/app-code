# Context

## Domain Terms

- `User`: A person using the app to train, track progress, and follow coaching content.
- `Coach`: A human who reviews user activity, answers messages, and manages content.
- `Admin`: A privileged operator who manages users, programs, offers, tickets, and internal tools.
- `Subscription Tier`: The access level for a user, ordered from `free` to `elite`.
- `Program`: A structured training plan made up of weeks and days.
- `Workout`: A single training session that may belong to a program or be run on its own.
- `Becoming Stage`: The app's progression model for user consistency and activity.
- `Support Thread`: A message thread used for user help, coaching, or app issues.
- `Legal Acceptance`: The set of terms, privacy, and disclaimer acknowledgements required before full app access.

## Product Notes

- The app blends training, nutrition, progress tracking, mindset, messaging, and subscription access.
- Higher tiers unlock more guidance, automation, and coaching touchpoints.
- Admin and coach capabilities are distinct from normal user capabilities.
